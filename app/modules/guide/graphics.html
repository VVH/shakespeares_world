Identifying Graphics

The Identify Graphic button appears in the left hand toolbar. It is used to describe any illustrations or marks which appear in the manuscript.

To mark a graphic, click on the Identify Graphic button , and drag your cursor, drawing a red shaded box around the object you wish to describe. This will open up a box titled “Select one graphic type.”

Coat of Arms
Flourish (used to describe doodles and other displays of penmanship)
Illustration (hand drawn or printed picture)
Decorated Initial
Dividing Line
Map
Manicule (pointing hand)
Ownership Mark
Seal
Symbol

Click on Save Changes before closing the box.

Depending on how many graphics appear in the document, you may need to insert multiple graphic boxes.

You can go back and edit or delete the Graphic description by hovering over the box with your cursor and selecting Edit or Delete.
